The Mustard Lady

The Mustard Lady is a small local business in the Comox Valley. It’s ran by Nancy who makes homemade mustard that she sells out of her brick-and-mortar store her current website isn’t as wholesome as she is, so I decided to redesign it. The new website has taken the brands original direction and changed it into something more bright and friendly. It has e-commerce functionality and blog set up for her recipes.
