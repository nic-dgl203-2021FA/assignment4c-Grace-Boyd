Roommatey

Roommatey is a pirate-themed roommate-finding app high fidelity wireframe completed in Adobe XD. It consists of 18 unique screens, some cool animations for carousel images and favoriting a house, and a lot of character; click around to see if you can find any buried treasure.

Borby Magazine

Borby Magazine is quite clearly a fictional magazine I designed for a younger audience (16+). Its content revolves around a game about dating pigeons, Borby mag has an emphasis on creating a fun and welcoming environment with its shapes colors, and even background elements.

Wild World

Wild World is a fictional magazine about nature and wildlife, it’s target audience is adults in their early 30’s. It focuses on creating a more welcoming but elegant design meant to draw in the said target audience. also included is an ad for ‘Comox Valley Bikes’ which is a fictional bike shop I designed a logo and advertisement for.

Savoury

Savory is a fictional food blog that I enhanced with JavaScript. The features I added were as follows Toggleable dark and light themes;A subscribe button in the menu is replaced with one of three custom messages; auto-fill users e-mail on the contact page if it's the user subscribes; and finally, slide show to the About page using jQuery

Coastal Campus Brewing

North island college’s very own brewing program asked the design students to do the branding for their up-and-coming beer. With a focus on natural elements around the island I designed two tall boy sleeves, a business card, a jacket & a full logo suite.

The Mustard Lady

The Mustard Lady is a small business local to the Comox Valley. It’s website isn’t as wholesome as the business is, so I decided to redesign it. The new website has taken the brands original direction and changed it into something more bright and friendly. It has e-commerce functionality and blog set up for her recipes.

About

As a design and development student, I’m learning new things every day and having fun doing it; I hear it never stops in this line of work.

I have an affinity for working with the physical and hope to get myself involved in some sort of packing/branding or alternatively with some sort of magazine. I also find myself interested in front-end development. Either way, I want to make fun designs for cool customers.
